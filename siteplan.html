Site Name

FabricCrafts — This name represents a creative website focused on DIY fabric projects, fabric recommendations, and fabric care tutorials. The name blends creativity with practicality for fabric lovers.

Site Purpose

The site will provide tutorials for sewing and fabric projects, fabric care guides, fabric product reviews, and a contact form for personalized tips or product inquiries.

Scenarios

  • What type of fabric is best for making summer dresses?
  • How do I properly wash and store silk or linen fabric?

Color Schema

  • Soft Rose (#E1A6AD): Used for headings and buttons.
  • Cream White (#FFF9F0): Used as the background for the whole document.

Typography

  • Playfair Display – Used for headings and titles to give a creative, elegant look.
  • Roboto – Used for paragraphs and body text for better readability.
