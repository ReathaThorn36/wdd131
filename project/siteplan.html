Fabric Store Website Plan

Created by Reatha Thorn

Site Name

Fabric Threads - This name is relevant and available for use. It reflects the fabric-based content of the website.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to share high-quality, affordable fabric products to customers who love DIY clothing and crafts. It will also provide educational content about fabric care and trends.

Target Audience

Our target audience includes DIY enthusiasts, fashion students, home decor crafters, and small tailoring businesses. The site will be easy to navigate for all age groups and optimized for both desktop and mobile users.

Color Scheme

We will use a clean, modern color palette:

  • Primary: #3B3B98 (Deep Blue)
  • Secondary: #F8A5C2 (Light Pink)
  • Accent: #63cdda (Light Teal)
  • Text: #333333 (Dark Gray)
  • Background: #ffffff (White)

Typography

Primary font: Roboto from Google Fonts for readability and clean design.
